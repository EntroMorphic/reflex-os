# Next Actions: The GOOSE Frontiers (v2.1)

This document outlines the high-order architectural and research frontiers identified after the successful implementation of **Geometric Arcing**, **Spatial Paging**, and **Geometric Flow**.

---

## 1. The Intelligence Boundary (Geometric AI)
**Observation:** We can route values, flows, and arcs. Can we route **Weights**? 
**Goal:** Implement a **Ternary Neural Field**.
- **Synaptic Routes:** Fragments that perform ternary multiplication and accumulation in the Loom.
- **Self-Regulating Weights:** Use the Supervisor to "train" the geometry based on physical feedback loops.

## 2. The Persistence Boundary (Live Manifest OTA)
**Observation:** Currently, the "Weave" is fixed at boot. 
**Goal:** Implement **Geometric Hot-Swapping**.
- **OTA Manifests:** Send new `.goose` manifests over the Atmosphere (Arcing) and weave them into the live Loom without a reboot.
- **Spatial Atomic Updates:** Ensure that re-weaving doesn't cause a Geometric Storm.

## 3. The Structural Boundary (Recursive Fields)
**Observation:** Fields are currently top-level expanses. 
**Goal:** Enable **Manifolds-within-Manifolds**.
- **Nested Loom:** A Cell in Field A can manifest as an entire Field B.
- **Hierarchical Governance:** Authority tilts that cascade down through nested manifolds.

## 4. The Signal Boundary (Ternary DSP)
**Observation:** DMA moves blocks, but we still need to process them ternarily.
**Goal:** Implement **Ternary Stream Operators**.
- Fragments for Ternary FFT, Convolution, and Filter patterns that operate at the speed of the **Geometric Flow**.
