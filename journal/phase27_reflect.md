# Reflections: Phase 27 (Geometric AI)

## Core Insight
Geometric AI is not "Math." It is **Self-Sculpting Topology.** The "Mind" of the machine is the shape of its connections. To think is to **Vibrate** the Loom in a specific pattern.

## Resolved Tensions
- **Precision vs Speed → Resolution:** We will use **Probabilistic Thresholds.** A "Neuron Cell" doesn't flip at exactly +3. It has an internal "Inertia Trit." Every incoming Pulse charges the inertia. This makes the AI resistant to noise but incredibly fast once the "Signal Wave" builds up.
- **Backpropagation vs Supervisor Rebalancing → Resolution:** We will implement **Topological Plasticity.** When `sys.ai.pain` is at -1, the Supervisor randomly "Jiggles" (flips 0 to 1 or 1 to -1) the orientation of the least-used routes. If the pain decreases, the change is "Pinned." This is **Evolutionary Machine Learning** at the register level.

## Hidden Assumptions
- **Assumption:** AI is for processing data.
- **Challenge:** On an ESP32-C6, "data" is often just a physical voltage.
- **New Understanding:** Geometric AI is the bridge between **Voltage and Intent.** It turns "High Voltage on Pin 5" into "Aggressive Posture in the Swarm" without a single line of binary logic in the middle.

## What I Now Understand
Phase 27 is the transition from **Programmed Machine** to **Learning Organism.** We aren't building a neural net *on* the OS; we are making the OS *be* the neural net.
